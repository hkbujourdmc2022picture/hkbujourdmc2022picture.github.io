Shenzhen Bay Quality Development Circle

Comprising Nanshan and Qianhai district, two of the nation's richest districts. Home to tech giants Tencent, Huawei and conglomerates like Ping’an.
Interfacing with the Yuen Long New Town, the Tin Shui Wai New Town, in which the New Territories North Central Business District is set to share opportunities with Shenzhen.

Hong Kong-Shenzhen Close Interaction Circle

The core region for future joint I&T industry development between Hong Kong and Shenzhen, with San Tin Technopole as major construction.
Here, San Tin/ Lok Ma Chau and Fanling/Sheung Shui New Town directly connect with Futian and Luohu district of Shenzhen through cross-boundary railway lines, an extension of the current MTR Northern Link.

Mirs Bay/Yan Chau Tong Eco-recreation/tourism Circle

A region abundant with nature reserves, marine parks and geoparks, aiming to provide leisure and green tourism for residents of the two cities.
